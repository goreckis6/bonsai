## FAQ

### What are the most low‑maintenance houseplants for beginners in 2024?
Species such as **ZZ plant**, **Snake Plant**, and **Pothos** require minimal watering and tolerate a range of light conditions. Their robust root systems make them forgiving of occasional neglect, ideal for first‑time owners.

### How can I enhance air quality with Millenials Houseplants 2024?
Select proven air‑purifying varieties like **Spider Plant**, **Peace Lily**, and **Boston Fern**. Position them near ventilation sources to maximize airflow through foliage, thereby increasing pollutant absorption.

### Are there any toxic plants I should avoid in a millennial apartment?
Yes; plants like **Oleander**, **Dieffenbachia**, and **Peace Lily** contain compounds harmful if ingested. When shared with pets or frequent guests, opt for non‑toxic alternatives such as **Cast Iron Plant** or **Calathea**.

### How often should I water succulents in a small apartment?
Succulents typically require watering once every 2–3 weeks, allowing the soil to dry completely between applications. Over‑watering is a primary cause of root rot, so always check moisture levels before irrigating.

### Can smart sensors replace traditional plant care routines?
Smart sensors provide valuable data, but they complement rather than replace hands‑on observation. Sensors alert users to moisture deficits and light shortages, enabling timely interventions while still encouraging personal engagement with plant health.
